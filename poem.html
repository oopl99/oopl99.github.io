Poem
We have created a poem which we believe portrays our opinion and emotions regarding the death penalty. By reading this poem you will hopefully better understand our position and join our cause.

A shot shatters the early morning silence
A man collapses, his life oozing through his chest
The crimson slows its relentless spread
as the officers race in for the arrest
And with them comes justice.
THe gavel comes down on the bench
the verdict comes, "Guilty, of murder in the first"
He struggles as they lead him away, pinioned by guards,
his mind races, he fears for the worst,
for he does not know when he will live or die.
The death penalty must be restored
for without it we pour our money down the drain
We waste our money, time, and people on prisoners
who should be dead to pay for the pain
they caused-this is the justice they deserve.
Our system as slow as time itself-
we must bring forth some speed
and carry out the executions because
we use our resources on those without need-
wasted on those whose lives mean nothing.
They are in prison for a good reason-
they have committed vile acts,
so we must pursue justice no matter the costs.
Now it's time to face the facts-
we cannot leave murderers alive.
